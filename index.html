Unveil the bamboo|curtain

bamboo｜curtain is first and foremost a platform for sharing and exploring ideas about how to improve tourism industries around the world. Specifically through language and cultural boundaries that come with a fast globalizing world.

Mandarin Chinese

It is a platform for tourism service providers to acquire the basics in Mandarin Chinese. This platform focuses on simple and efficient language training directed towards people working in the tourism sector.

Service providers will then be better equipped in providing better service to travelers from China and East-Asian countries. From travel agents, flight attendants to bus drivers and tour guides, all these people are the face of the company and the service they provide determines the success of the business.

Provide For the Future

Every year, the number of Mandarin Chinese speaking travelers increases multifold and by 2030, two-thirds of the global middle class will be living in Asia. In the next 10 years, the number of middle-class income earners will balloon five times up to 3.2 billion and will be the biggest group of consumers that companies should take notice of.
